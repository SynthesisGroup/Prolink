Our story

Providing Expert I.T. Services Since 1999.

In 1999, Brian Shad began building ProLink, now a full-service consulting and IT outsourcing firm. ProLink is Brian’s direct response to the need for high-value consulting and IT outsourcing services, driven by results. By attracting and retaining top talent—project managers, consultants, developers, and engineers—with a focus on excellent customer service, ProLink has grown rapidly and realized Brian’s vision in the communities it serves.

Nearly 20 years later, we still go out of our way to carefully monitor our clients’ infrastructures, networks, and Cloud environments like we’re sitting in the next cube. With our headquarters in Los Angeles, today we manage and support many clients in California, New York, Texas, Philippines and Mexico. Throughout the time, we have stayed true to our dedication to outstanding customer service and the values on which ProLink was built on: trust, security, integrity, quality, respect and customer commitment. We’re proud to be collaborators and problem solvers.
