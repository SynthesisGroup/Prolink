Our story

Providing Expert I.T. Services Since 1999.

In 1999, Brian Shad began building ProLink, now a full-service consulting and IT outsourcing firm. ProLink is Brian’s direct response to the need for high-value consulting and IT outsourcing services, driven by results. By attracting and retaining top talent—project managers, consultants, developers, and engineers—with a focus on excellent customer service, ProLink has grown rapidly and realized Brian’s vision in the communities it serves.

Nearly 20 years later, we still go out of our way to carefully monitor our clients’ infrastructures, networks, and Cloud environments like we’re sitting in the next cube. With our headquarer in Los Angeles, today we manage and suppoer many clients in California, New York, Texas, Phillipines and Mexico. Throughout the time, we have stayed true to our dedication to outstanding customer service and the values on which ProLink was built on: trust, security, integrity, quality, respect and customer commitment. We’re proud to be collaborators and problem solvers.

SERVER MANAGEMENT

Servers are among the most vital parts of any network. They hold the keys to your network environment, host your applications, send and receive emails, maintain company documents, file structure and databases and much more. Proper management of servers is essential to maintaining a healthy production environment within any business computer network.

ProLink's Server management involves core administration and maintenance of both the physical hardware of Servers as well as management of the various roles and applications that the Server is hosting. ProLink offers the most complete set of services in properly maintaining and managing your servers which include Server installation, upgrade, migration and virtualization on all platforms including Windows, Linux and Mac.
