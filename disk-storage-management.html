Disk and Storage Management

Disk and Storage Management throughout the network is an important part of the overall management of the network. By utilizing smart agents and monitoring tools, ProLink support team can keep an eye on every disk and storage location throughout the network. Each disk on every server or Network Attached Storages (NAS) are monitored 24/7, by looking all aspects of the disks in question to ensure proper storage and availability of data.

Using smart utilities and agents, ProLink engineers get real-time storage visibility into any Disk, NAS or SAN arrays and can respond to any Disk Percentage, Disk Health or any other alerts to ensure peak performance and avoid capacity surprises.
